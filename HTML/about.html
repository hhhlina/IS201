About Me

This is a little glimpse into who I am.

I was born in Seoul, South Korea and then moved to the States in 2007. I love listening to music, reading, watching random Youtube videos, and sleeping! My favorite season is spring or fall because I can't stand it if it is too hot or too cold so these two seasons feel perfect.

I am also an avid fan of KPOP, and more specifically a group called SEVENTEEN. I enjoy watching their skilled performances and also their appearances on variety shows. They never fail to make me laugh and one of my greatest wishes at the moment is to go to one of their concerts and see them perform in person.
